Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how onePageApps.gic.mx ("the Site", "we", "us") handles information when you visit the site or use the companion iOS/macOS app. We are committed to keeping this simple and transparent.

2. Information We Collect

The Site is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not operate databases or user accounts. The only data collection occurs through:

  • Analytics (optional): If Google Analytics is enabled, it collects anonymised page-view data (page URL, referrer, browser type, country). No personally identifiable information is associated with these events.
  • GitHub Pages server logs: GitHub may collect standard web server logs (IP address, browser, timestamp) as described in GitHub's privacy statement.

3. Cookies

The Site itself does not set any first-party cookies. Google Analytics (if enabled) may set third-party cookies. You can block these using your browser settings or a content blocker.

4. Third-Party Services

Some apps in the library make calls to third-party APIs (e.g. OpenAI) when you deploy and use them. Any data sent to those APIs is governed by their own privacy policies. The Site itself does not proxy, store, or transmit your API keys.

5. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. Analytics data retained by Google Analytics follows Google's standard retention policy (26 months by default, adjustable in the property settings).

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have rights to access, correct, or delete data held about you. Because we hold no personal data, most of these rights have no practical effect here. For analytics opt-out, use Google's opt-out browser add-on.

7. Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Please contact us via GitHub.
